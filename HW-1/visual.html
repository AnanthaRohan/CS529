The Oldest Persons In The World

While surfing through the articles to look for data-driven visualization articles, this particular article "Why The Oldest Person In The World Keeps Dying" from 2015 grabbed my attention. This chart not only shows when a person was consider to be the oldest but also shows at what age that person passed away. I liked this visualization as it is unique in its own way and at the same time represents a lot of information. Just through a simple graphical line with a bubble end, each of these are identified as an individual. Identifing successor for the oldest person when the current oldest person passes away is easy to identified through this representation. The color scheme used also makes it convinent for the user as their high value difference in contrast between foreground and background.

It would have been better if the Y-axis had age above 115yrs old, as it would let the viewer easily identify the ages of the oldest persons post 1990s. This could done by repositioning the "Key" box towards right.
